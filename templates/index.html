Welcome to the Electronic Health Management System (EHRMS)

Briefly introduce the system as a platform that simplifies healthcare record-keeping and appointment management for both patients and doctors. Highlight the project's goal of digitizing health records and streamlining doctor-patient interactions

Instructions for Patients

As a patient, you can easily manage your personal health records using the Electronic Health Record Management System. Follow the steps below to get started:

Step 1: Create Your Account

Register by providing your Aadhaar number, username, and password. Ensure your details are accurate for seamless access.

Step 2: Store Personal Records

After registration, you can securely store all your personal and medical information, including medical history, current medications, and allergies.

Step 3: Maintain Health Records

Regularly update your health records after each medical visit. This includes new diagnoses, medications prescribed, and follow-up appointments.

Step 4: View Your Records

Easily access and review your health records at any time. This helps you keep track of your health status and share important information with your healthcare providers.

Instructions for Doctors

Follow the steps below to effectively use the Electronic Health Record Management System:

Step 1: Login & Access Dashboard

Doctors can log in using their assigned credentials (Doctor ID and password). Once logged in, you will be directed to your dashboard.

Step 2: Manage Appointments

View and manage your appointments from the dashboard. You'll have access to patient details, appointment statuses, and any rescheduling options available.

Step 3: Update Patient Records

After consultations, update patient records with necessary information such as diagnosis, prescribed medications, and follow-up instructions to ensure continuity of care.

Step 4: View Feedback

Review patient feedback on your consultations to enhance your services and improve patient satisfaction.

How to Book an Appointment

1. Login to Your Account

Login using your Patient ID, Aadhaar number, and password. You will be redirected to your dashboard after successful login.

2. Select Doctor & Time Slot

Go to the 'Book Appointment' section, select a doctor by specialization or hospital, and choose a time slot that works for you.

3. Confirm Appointment

Review the selected time and date, confirm the booking, and an Appointment ID will be generated. You can view the appointment details on your dashboard.
